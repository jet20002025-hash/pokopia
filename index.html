About the Game

🎮 Game Overview

Pokémon Pokopia is the first life simulation game in the Pokémon series, developed by The Pokémon Company and GAME FREAK. Players take on the role of a Ditto that has transformed into a human, collecting wood, rocks, and other materials to build a cozy home while learning skills from other Pokémon to create your very own Pokémon paradise.

📅 Release Information

Release Date: 2026
Platform: Nintendo Switch 2 Exclusive
Developer: The Pokémon Company / GAME FREAK
Genre: Life Simulation
Players: 1-4
Rating: RP (Rating Pending)

🌟 Core Gameplay

Experience slow-paced building life! Use Bulbasaur's Leafage to add greenery to the landscape, learn Squirtle's Water Gun to hydrate plants. Build furniture, grow vegetables, create homes for Pokémon, and attract more Pokémon visitors by creating a lush, welcoming environment.
